A routine for each project means consistency and quality.

It starts with the first estimate. We look at the project in hand and estimate our price by calculating material and labor costs. If you have any questions we will gladly answer them.

The preparation process is as important as the service itself. We will cover furniture, windows, tables, fix damaged walls or ceilings, sand down doors, etc.

Now the real work begins. We carefully paint your desired space. We ensure that we use the paint effectively and efficiently.

The final inspection begins. We check every wall, every corner to ensure a proper paint job. We show you the results and adjust according to your assessment.

After our work surpasses your expectations, we begin to clean up. This means uncovering furniture, putting furniture back in place, final touch ups, etc.

All that remains is a satisfied customer and a brand new house.
