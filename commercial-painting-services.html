Painting Services for Commercial Spaces

Keeping businesses looking professional

Our commercial painting services. Made with your business in mind.

Interior Painting

Provide a fresh, vibrant, and professional look to interior surfaces, contributing to an inviting and appealing atmosphere for employees, clients, and visitors.

We also provide special services for those in need of a unique approach to their project:

Special finishes

Offering techniques like faux painting, texture application, stenciling, and murals to create unique visual effects.

Epoxy coating

Installing durable and decorative epoxy coatings for garage floors and basements.

Texture Application

Adding texture to walls or cielings for a unique look or to hide imperfections.

Exterior Painting

Enhance the building's curb appeal but also protect against weather-related damage, and moisture. Helps contribute to creating a professional and inviting appearance.

We also provide special services for those in need of a unique approach to their project:

Deck and fence staining

Stain and seal decks and fences to help preserve and enhance outdoor space.

Pressure Washing

Cleaning exterior surfaces to remove dirt, grime, and mildew before painting.

Caulking and sealing

Ensure 100% waterproofing and weather proofing of doors, windows, siding, and trim for a complete finish.

A routine for each project means consistency and quality.

It starts with the first estimate. We look at the project in hand and estimate our price by calculating material and labor costs. If you have any questions we will gladly answer them.

The preparation process is as important as the service itself. We will cover furniture, windows, carpets, fix damaged walls or ceilings, sand down doors, etc.

Now the real work begins. We carefully paint your desired rooms. We ensure that we use the paint effectively and efficiently.

The final inspection begins. We check every wall, every corner to ensure a proper paint job. We show you the results and adjust according to your assessment.

After our work surpasses your expectations, we begin to clean up. This means uncovering furniture, putting furniture back in place, final touch ups, etc.

All that remains is a satisfied customer and a brand new workspace.
