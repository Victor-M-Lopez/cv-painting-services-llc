A routine for each project means consistency and quality.

It starts with the first estimate. We look at the project in hand and estimate our price by calculating material and labor costs. If you have any questions we will gladly answer them.

The preparation process is as important as the service itself. We will cover furniture, windows, carpets, commercial furniture, anything that could get in the way of the repairing process.

Now the real work begins. We repair the requested area We ensure that the repair not only fixes the issue, but that it also lasts a very long time.

The final inspection begins. We test the repaired area, look for any new problems, making sure that our repair fixed the problem, etc.

After our work surpasses your expectations, we begin to clean up. This means uncovering furniture, putting furniture back in place, final touch ups, etc.

All that remains is a satisfied customer and a brand new workspace.
